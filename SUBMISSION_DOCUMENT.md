## 📌 Executive Summary
**Bazaar Brain** is a next-generation **AI-Driven Risk Orchestration Dashboard** designed for the Indian Stock Market (NSE). While traditional trading apps rely on probabilistic "guesses" or simple technical indicators, Bazaar Brain introduces **formal verification** into the trading loop. ## 🚀 The Core Innovation: "The Verifiable AI"
The biggest problem with AI in finance is **trust**. LLMs can hallucinate; they can misinterpret data or provide "gut-feeling" predictions.

### How Bazaar Brain is Different:
1.  **The Auditor (Z3 SMT Solver)**: Before the AI speaks, our **Z3 Risk Engine** translates market data into formal logical formulas. It checks for specific "Unsafe States" (e.g., flash crashes, volume-price divergence, or cross-sector contagion).
2.  **The Brain (Gemini Pro)**: Once a risk or opportunity is *mathematically proven*, Gemini Pro interprets the context and generates a "Market Directive"—a clear, human-readable instruction.
3.  **The Proof**: Every directive carries a verification record. If the math doesn't add up, the AI stays silent. This is the **"Proof-over-Prediction"** model.

## 💎 Key Features & Innovations

### 1. 3D Nerve Center (War Room UI)

### 2. Hindi Voice Semantic Layer
To empower the **Next 100 Million** Indian investors, Bazaar Brain is built vocal-first.
*   **Gemini-Powered Intent**: Users can speak natural Hindi (e.g., *"Banking sector ka haal kya hai?"*).
*   **Direct Action**: The system parses the intent, filters the data through the Z3 Auditor, and responds instantly with visual and audio feedback.

### 3. Sentinel: Cross-Sector Contagion Oracle
Sentinel monitors the **correlative stress** between 8 major NSE sectors.
*   **Predictive Risk**: If stress in the Banking sector begins leaking into IT or Auto, Sentinel fires a "Pre-Shock" warning.
*   **The Logic**: It uses a rolling temporal correlation matrix to find hidden lead-lag relationships that traditional charts miss.

### 4. 3-Tier Data Waterfall
Bazaar Brain is built for **zero downtime**.
- **Tier 1**: Real-time NSE Official feed.
- **Tier 2**: Yahoo Finance sub-second fallback.
- **Tier 3**: **Micro-Jitter Cache** – Our server maintains a state-space simulation to keep the risk engine active even if external APIs are throttled or during market holidays.
